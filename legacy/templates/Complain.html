📂 How to Upload Media Evidence

If you have photos or video evidence, upload them to Google Drive and paste the shared link in the form. Follow these quick steps:

1

Create a Folder

Open Google Drive and create a new folder named "SCMGS".

2

Upload Files

Upload images/videos from your phone or desktop to the folder.

3

Share link

Right-click or tap settings, click Share, set access to "Anyone with the link", and copy it.

4

Paste Link

Paste the copied share link into the "Upload Drive Link" field on the report form.
